RICE-BRAN

A nutrient-rich byproduct of rice milling, ideal for animal feed and soil health.

  • ✅ **High Energy Feed** – Boosts weight gain in poultry, pigs, goats, and cattle.
  • ✅ **Rich in Protein & Fats** – Supports animal growth and productivity.
  • ✅ **Affordable & Available** – Great substitute for costly feeds.
  • ✅ **Enhances Digestion** – Contains essential fiber for livestock gut health.
  • ✅ **Natural Fertilizer** – When composted, enriches soil and boosts crop yield.
  • ✅ **Eco-Friendly** – A sustainable use of rice milling waste.

RICE-HUSK

A highly versatile by-product of rice milling, known for industrial and agricultural value.

Benefits and Uses of Rice Husk

  • Fuel Source: Used in biomass boilers and power plants for clean energy.
  • Soil Amendment: Improves aeration and water retention in sandy soils.
  • Animal Bedding: Comfortable, absorbent, and cost-effective bedding for livestock.
  • Building Material: Utilized in eco-bricks, cement production, and insulation panels.
  • Biochar Production: When carbonized, turns into biochar for enriching soil and carbon sequestration.
  • Silica Extraction: Contains high silica content used in glass, ceramics, and electronics.
  • Composting: Enhances microbial activity and organic matter in compost systems.
  • Eco-Friendly: Reduces agricultural waste and supports circular economy practices.

CARBONATED-RICE-HUSK / BIOCHAR

A high-carbon material created through pyrolysis, ideal for soil health, carbon sequestration, and environmental use.

Benefits and Uses of Carbonated Rice Husk / Biochar

  • Soil Enrichment: Improves soil structure, retains nutrients and moisture, and supports microbial life.
  • Carbon Sequestration: Captures carbon and stores it in the soil for hundreds of years — fighting climate change.
  • Water Retention: Increases water-holding capacity in sandy or dry soils.
  • Waste Management: Turns agricultural waste into valuable, eco-friendly material.
  • Livestock Feed Additive: Can reduce methane emissions and improve animal digestion in small doses.
  • Odor Control: Used in composting, poultry farms, and animal bedding to absorb ammonia and reduce smells.
  • Filtration: Acts as a natural filter for water purification due to its porous structure.
  • Eco-Friendly Fuel: Burned as a cleaner biomass fuel source.

CHARCOAL

A time-tested fuel and multipurpose material used across households, agriculture, industry, and medicine.

Benefits and Uses of Charcoal

  • Efficient Fuel: Burns hotter and longer than wood, perfect for cooking and heating.
  • Water Purification: Activated charcoal filters impurities and chemicals from drinking water.
  • Air Deodorizer: Absorbs odors in homes, fridges, and shoes naturally.
  • Soil Amendment: Improves drainage and nutrient retention when used in gardens and pots.
  • Metal Smelting: Used in small-scale blacksmithing and traditional metallurgy.
  • Animal Feed Additive: Aids digestion and toxin absorption in livestock diets.
  • Natural Medicine: Used for detox, anti-poison treatment, and digestive relief in activated form.
  • Eco-Friendly Waste Use: Recycled from agricultural residues into sustainable energy and materials.
